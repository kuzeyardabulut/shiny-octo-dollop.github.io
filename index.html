Welcome to HexHack!

HexHack is a conference and community organized by young security researchers from around the world. We come together to learn, share, and have fun while exploring the frontiers of applied security.

Our team explores and shares work in web security, pwn (binary exploitation), and reverse engineering — all while keeping the vibe friendly, curious, and community-driven.

Join the community

What do we do?

HexHack regularly shares research insights, write-ups, and tools produced by our members. We host community meetups, workshops, and informal capture-the-flag sessions so people at every level can learn and collaborate.

Whether you're just starting out or you're an experienced researcher, there's a place for you here — we encourage sharing, mentorship, and open discussion.

Web Security

Research and tooling for securing web applications: XSS, CSRF, auth issues, secure headers, and modern web attack surfaces.

Blockchain Security

Audits, smart contract analysis, and research into consensus, DeFi exploits, and secure on-chain patterns.

Pwn & Exploitation

Binary exploitation, CTF write-ups, fuzzing results, and techniques for finding and exploiting memory bugs.

Reverse Engineering

Static and dynamic analysis, tooling tips, unpacking strategies, and reversing challenges across platforms.

Cloud Security

Research into misconfigurations, privilege escalation, container/container-orchestration security, and secure cloud practices.

Malware Analysis

Behavioral analysis, sandboxing techniques, indicators-of-compromise, and research on modern malware families.
